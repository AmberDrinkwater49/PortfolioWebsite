Mini Solar Panel Replacement Robot

RBE 2001 - Manipulation centered around creating 4-bar systems to manipulate objects. In the final project I designed and printed an actuation system to place “solar panels” onto mini “roofs.” While this was a mockup, much of the calculations we had to do were the same as if it were a real system. One of the issues of note is that one of the “solar panels” was about 1 kg in weight, compared to the robot which was at most half that. This meant that our mechanical advantage had to be considerable in order to lift the panel.

Moreover, I programmed the robot to move along lines and go through a state machine to complete the task. Images and videos of the robot operating are attached below.
